According to the publication "Ankit. (2025). Heart Attack Risk & Prediction Dataset In India [Data set]. Kaggle. https://doi.org/10.34740/KAGGLE/DSV/10853291", cardiovascular diseases are the leading cause of death in India.

The dataset associated with the publication provides extensive information on individuals’ health across various Indian states. In particular, it focuses on risk factors that are presumed to contribute to the onset of heart attacks.

The dataset used in this project was downloaded from Kaggle. Therefore, please refer to the corresponding publication for further details.
