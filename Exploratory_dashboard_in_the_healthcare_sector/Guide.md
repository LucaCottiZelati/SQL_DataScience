## Dashboard on Cardiovascular Risk Factors

### Description
This dashboard explores the main factors associated with cardiovascular risk within a patient sample, providing a concise yet interactive view of the population’s demographic, clinical, and behavioral profile. The visualization combines demographic indicators, lifestyle-related variables, cardiovascular risk distribution, and key average clinical parameters such as LDL, HDL, and blood pressure.

### Objective
The purpose of this dashboard is to support an exploratory analysis of the dataset, helping identify patterns that may clarify how personal characteristics, clinical conditions, and lifestyle habits are associated with cardiovascular risk. It is designed to enable quick data reading and immediate comparisons across patient subgroups.

### Dashboard Contents
The dashboard opens with an overview including:

- total number of patients
- average age
- median age

It also includes sections dedicated to:

- patient distribution by gender
- presence of categories or conditions such as athletes, smokers, diabetic patients, hypertensive patients, obese patients, and other behavioral or clinical profiles
- cardiovascular risk analysis
- identification of values potentially associated with cardiovascular risk
- comparison of average clinical values between female and male patients for LDL, HDL, systolic blood pressure, and diastolic blood pressure

### Filters and Interactivity
The dashboard includes interactive filters that dynamically update charts and indicators, making it possible to perform more targeted analyses on specific segments of the observed population.

### Insights
Through this dashboard, it is possible to:

- compare the average clinical profile between men and women
- observe the distribution of cardiovascular risk across classes or groups
- assess the impact of known risk factors such as smoking, obesity, hypertension, and diabetes
- quickly analyze the main demographic characteristics of the sample

### Data Source
The dashboard is based on the following dataset:

**Ankit. (2025). _Heart Attack Risk & Prediction Dataset In India_ [Data set]. Kaggle. DOI: 10.34740/KAGGLE/DSV/10853291.**

### Possible Uses
This dashboard can be useful in contexts such as:

- data visualization portfolio projects
- exploratory analysis in the healthcare domain
- business intelligence and data storytelling exercises
- educational or demonstration presentations based on clinical datasets

### Note
This dashboard is intended for exploratory and descriptive purposes only. Any patterns highlighted in the charts do not, by themselves, constitute clinical or predictive validation, but rather support dataset interpretation and the identification of trends that may be explored further through statistical analysis or dedicated predictive models.
